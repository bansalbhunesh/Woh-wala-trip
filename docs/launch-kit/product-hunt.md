# Product Hunt Launch Kit

## Tagline

"Your friend group's trip photos, turned into a cinematic documentary by AI."

## Short Description (60 chars)

"AI turns your trips into mythology."

## Description

Yaarlore transforms your travel photos into mythology. Upload your group trip photos and the AI generates:

- Character roles (Chaos Source, Golden Retriever, NPC Energy, Plot Twist...)
- Chaos scores — ranked from Chill to Certified Unhinged
- Trip eras (named documentary chapters)
- A cinematic story you'll actually want to share

India-first. Built for the chaos of Indian friend groups that no Western app captures.

Google Photos gives you a slideshow. Yaarlore gives you mythology.

## Platforms to Pre-Promote

- Twitter/X: thread on "AI turns your group trip into a documentary"
- Instagram: Reels showing the lore generation process
- LinkedIn: founder story angle (India-first product, not a Spotify Wrapped clone)
- WhatsApp: share your own trip documentary link with one group to seed virality

## Metrics to Highlight on Launch Day

- Avg chaos score across all trips
- % of trips rated "Certified Unhinged"
- Most common character archetype assigned
- Hall of Chaos #1 trip chaos score

## Anti-positioning

This is NOT:

- A Google Photos competitor (we don't store your photos)
- A Spotify Wrapped clone (we analyze chaos, not stats)
- A travel app (we document friendships, not destinations)

This IS:

- The first AI friendship mythology platform
- A documentary generator, not a slideshow maker
- India's answer to the "gap year travel blog" era

## Pricing Framing

- Free: First trip is always free. Generate lore, share the story.
- Paid: Unlock unlimited trips, digital exports, print books.
- Pitch: "First trip's on us. After that, decide if your group's chaos is worth immortalizing."
