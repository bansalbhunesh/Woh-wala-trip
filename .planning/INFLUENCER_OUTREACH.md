# Yaarlore Influencer Outreach

## Target Profile

**Platform:** Instagram (primary), YouTube (secondary)
**Follower range:** 10K–200K
**Age:** 22–30
**Content:** Indian friend group travel — Himachal, Goa, Rajasthan, Northeast
**Vibe:** Candid, roasty, "we almost died but it was worth it" energy

Avoid: solo travelers, luxury travel, travel photographers (wrong use case)

---

## DM Template (Instagram, under 200 words)

```
Hey [Name],

Saw your [specific trip] reel — [specific moment you noticed, e.g. "the bit where you all got lost at 2am"] was actually hilarious.

Built something that might be embarrassingly relevant: Yaarlore generates an AI trip documentary for friend groups — cinematic lore, character roles ("The Chaos Agent", "The Reluctant Planner"), a chaos score, and shareable story cards.

Demo (no signup, 30 seconds): yaarlore.com/demo

We're giving 10 creators free lifetime access in exchange for one honest post — whether it's good, bad, or "our trip got a 92 chaos score and now everyone's fighting about it."

No script, no branded content agreement, no requirement to say it's good. Just use it on a real trip and share if you want.

Interested?

[Your name]
```

**Personalization rule:** Never send without filling in [specific trip] and [specific moment]. Generic outreach bounces.

## Offer Structure

**What you give:**

- Free lifetime Pro access (unlimited generations, all features)
- Early access to Yearly Wrap before public launch
- Listing on a "Founding Creators" page (social proof for them)

**What you ask:**

- Use Yaarlore on one real trip
- Share if they want to (zero obligation)
- One DM back with feedback (good, bad, or ugly)

**What you DO NOT do:**

- Require a post
- Require positive framing
- Ask for link in bio (they'll do it if they love it)

## What "Going Viral" Looks Like

The ideal creator post:

1. Shows their group's lore output (chaos score, one character card)
2. Tags the friends in it (drives secondary shares)
3. Has a moment of "okay this is embarrassingly accurate"

The viral trigger: the friends reacting to their character roles. That's the screenshot moment.

**Best performing lore for screenshots:** Chaos scores above 80, character roles with "Chaos Agent" or "The One Who Made That Sound The Car Made."
